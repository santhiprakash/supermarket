# Product Requirements Document (Initial Draft)

## Vision
Build a modern supermarket website that enables customers to browse products, place online orders, discover promotions, and increase visits to the physical store.

## Business Goals
- Increase online sales
- Increase store footfall
- Provide a seamless omnichannel experience

## Target Users
- Families
- Working professionals
- Students
- Senior citizens
- Small businesses

## MVP Features
- Home page
- Product catalog
- Categories
- Product search
- Product details
- Cart
- Checkout
- Address management
- User accounts
- Order tracking
- Offers & coupons
- Click & Collect
- Home delivery
- Contact & Store information
- Admin dashboard
- Inventory management
- Order management

## Future Enhancements
- Multi-store
- Loyalty program
- AI recommendations
- Subscription orders
- Mobile apps
